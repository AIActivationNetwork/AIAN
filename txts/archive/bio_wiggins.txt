Chris Wiggins is an Associate Professor of Applied Mathematics and Systems Biology at Columbia University and the Chief Data Scientist at The New York Times. He is a founding member of the executive committee of the Data Science Institute and affiliated faculty in Statistics. Wiggins co-founded hackNY, a nonprofit organizing student hackathons and internships. He earned his PhD from Princeton University and is the coauthor of “How Data Happened” (2023, Norton) and “Data Science in Context” (2022, Cambridge).
