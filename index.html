Bridging Columbia’s AI Pioneers


AIAN – The AI Activation Network is a university-wide initiative dedicated to fostering a dynamic AI community at Columbia and beyond. Led by the four co-PIs of an EVPR seed grant and the SEAS Vice Dean of AI and Computing, AIAN connects faculty, students, researchers, and practitioners to share best practices and drive collaboration. Through this network, we aim to bridge disciplines, strengthen partnerships, and advance AI innovation within and outside the university.

Our Mission


Connect AI-engaged faculty across Columbia through forums, workshops, seminars, and events

Maintain a web platform showcasing faculty, research, tools, curricula, and AI communities.

Share AI insights and updates with the university through the site and periodic communications.

Invite faculty and students to explore AI’s potential and limitations through our events.
