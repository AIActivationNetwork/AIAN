Bridging Columbia’s AI Pioneers


AIAN – The AI Activation Network working group – is a university-wide initiative focused on building a dynamic network to connect researchers using and developing AI systems both within Columbia University and with an external community.

Conceived as a “centerless center,” AIAN’s mission is to spark and support new collaborative projects that combine AI with various research and creative practices across campus and beyond.

AIAN is not a formal center; rather, it provides essential infrastructure to surface emerging research directions, foster collaboration, and share outcomes.

By catalyzing introductions, student seed funding, convenings, and publications, AIAN offers everything a typical center would—but remains able to adapt to the unique personalities, goals, and research interests of its participants at any given time.

Our Mission


CONNECT
AI-engaged faculty across Columbia through forums, workshops, seminars, and events.

MAINTAIN
a web platform showcasing faculty, research, tools, curricula, and AI communities.

SHARE
AI insights and updates with the university through the site and periodic communications.

INVITE
faculty and students to explore AI’s potential and limitations through our events.
